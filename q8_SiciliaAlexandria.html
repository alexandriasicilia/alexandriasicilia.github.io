How do you attach an External Style Sheet to your webpage?

  1. <link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="mystyle.css">
  2. link <rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="mystyle.css" >
  3. <link rel="stylesheet"; type="text/css"; href="mystyle.css";>
  4. <link rel="stylesheet", type="text/css", href="mystyle.css";>
